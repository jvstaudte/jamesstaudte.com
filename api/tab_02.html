Tableau Visualation of Archibus Buildings

Archibus is an Integrated Workplace Management System used for managing space and floorplans. It ships with a sample database called HQ to demonstrate its capabilities. This visualization shows a map of all the buildings highlighted by their regions and the circles represeting the size (square footage) of the buildings. Below are the same buildings broken down into their Usage and Occupancy. Selecting a Region from the legend will highlight it in both the map and the table.
